Our Flagship Event

The Mastering Kicad Workshop was an interactive session where participants learned the fundamentals of designing and fabricating PCBs. The workshop typically covered various aspects of PCB design, including schematic capture, component placement, routing, design rules, and manufacturing considerations. The Bio Bits seminar was based on the 3D liver and liver tumour segmentation model building which the students found very fascinating.

Events

Step Up

The Step Up event covered four tracks—Career Elevation, Career Transition, Cybersecurity, and Blockchain—aimed at enhancing attendees' careers. Mr. Sanjay Jadhav began with insights on emerging engineering fields and essential skills, followed by Mr. Vinay Raikar, who discussed ethics and career transitions. Mr. Sahil Sawant led an interactive networking activity, and Tathagat Kumar shared his IEEE experiences. After lunch, Mr. Raghwan Sundarajan introduced blockchain, while Mr. Neeraj Kumar focused on cybersecurity and certifications.

Quarter Tech Talk

The IEEE Quarter Tech Talk Table 11.0, hosted at the VIT auditorium, was a full-day event focused on learning and discussing new technology. Host speakers were Ramnik Kalra and Nidhi Shingade. The discussion was broadcast via Google Meet, allowing more people to participate. Attendees were able to speak directly with specialists about volunteering, networking, and using IEEE services such as IEEE Xplore and IEEE TV. This workshop clarified how to succeed in the future, emphasizing the value of community and continuous learning.
